The production page is the center of batch execution.

## What this page is for

It tracks:

- batch status,
- production date,
- line,
- optional wave,
- production items,
- selected lots,
- readiness to start.

## Frequent question

### Why can I not start the production?

Usually because one or more blocking inputs are not allocated yet, or a business guard prevents the status transition.
