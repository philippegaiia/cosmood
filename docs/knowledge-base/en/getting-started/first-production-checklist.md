Before creating your first production, check the points below.

## Minimum checklist

- the product exists and is active,
- the product type is defined,
- a usable formula exists,
- critical ingredients are supplier-linked,
- allowed production lines make sense,
- you know whether this will be a standalone batch or a wave.

## Typical question

### Why does the app block me later?

Usually because a prerequisite was skipped early: product type, formula, allowed line, or supplier linkage.
