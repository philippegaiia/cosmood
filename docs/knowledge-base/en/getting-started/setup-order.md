This page explains the safest order for setting up data so later workflows stay smooth.

## Recommended order

1. ingredient categories,
2. ingredients,
3. suppliers,
4. supplier listings,
5. product categories,
6. production lines and holidays,
7. product types,
8. formulas,
9. QC templates and task templates,
10. products,
11. productions or waves.

## Why this matters

- A product depends on its product type.
- Useful supplier orders depend on ingredients already being supplier-linked.
- Productions are easier to plan when lines, templates, and QC rules already exist.
