The flash simulator estimates load and requirements quickly without reserving stock.

## What this page is for

It helps you:

- enter products and desired units,
- choose batch formats,
- estimate required batches,
- preview ingredient and packaging needs,
- estimate tasks and costs,
- convert a simulation into a wave later.

## Frequent question

### Does the simulator reserve stock?

No. It is planning-only until you convert the result into a real workflow.
