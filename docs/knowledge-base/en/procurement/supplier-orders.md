A supplier order turns a requirement into a real purchasing commitment.

## What this page is for

It manages:

- supplier,
- ordered lines,
- order status,
- delivery dates,
- quantities committed to a wave,
- the transition toward receipt and stock.

## Frequent question

### Does ordered mean covered?

Not completely. It helps planning first. Execution becomes truly secure only with real stock and then real allocations.
