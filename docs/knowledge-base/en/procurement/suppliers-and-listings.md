Suppliers and supplier listings make procurement usable in practice.

## What this page is for

It tells the system:

- who can supply what,
- in which purchasing format,
- with which usable reference,
- with which real content per unit.

## Common mistake

Confusing the business ingredient with the supplier-side purchasing listing.
