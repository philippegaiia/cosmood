The product page defines the SKU or manufactured item.

## What this page is for

It connects:

- product category,
- product type,
- net weight,
- default formula,
- packaging,
- launch date,
- active status.

## Frequent question

### Why is the product type so important here?

Because the product alone does not define planning logic.
